boostmyrevenue.net

BoostMyRevenue is a Service/Api for the software developers to distribute a percentage of their revenue among their users.

Main goals of this project:
Enabling software developers to share a percentage of their revenues among their users.
Collect user interactions and calculate their earnings by their interaction points.
Display analytical data and user trends to the developers/companies.
Encouraging end users to use a specific app that uses BoostMyRevenue service without encouraging them from clicking ads.
Promoting new BoostMyRevenue integrated apps to our existing users throw push notifications.
Getting more active users for the developers.
